Welcome to Biotech Orthomart

Biotech Orthomart serves as a specialized importer and distributor of high-quality orthopaedic implants, orthotic and prosthetic devices, surgical instruments, medical textiles, surgical imaging devices and theatre consumables including sterile disposable surgical packs and gowns. By leveraging Kampala’s logistics hubs, the company will meet Uganda’s growing demand for trauma and orthopaedic care.

With a dedicated team of qualified pharmacists and healthcare professionals, we ensure that every product you receive meets the highest standards of safety and effectiveness. We take pride in offering personalized advice, affordable prices, and a seamless shopping experience — both in-store and online.

Your well-being is our priority, and we are committed to walking with you on your journey to better health. Thank you for choosing Biotech Orthomart as your trusted healthcare partner.
